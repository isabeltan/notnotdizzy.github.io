Public Awareness

ux/ui, design studies

This is a series of screenshots of a website, which I built using HTML/CSS and JS for Design for Public Awareness. The objective of this project was to create a visual representation of our studio practice drawing from our identities and experiences. This website invites users to navigate through a range of audio, text, and images, sourced through lecture. I wanted to create something that felt living, through the interchanging images and addition of audio, and to add elements which challenged the idea of legibility, through the drop-down navigation whose relationship to the object it activated was arbitrary. The result is a space which encourages the user to explore and customize a myriad of different mashups.
